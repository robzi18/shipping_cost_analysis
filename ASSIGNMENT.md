# Assignment: Did Free Shipping Increase Profit?

## Background

You've just joined the data team at a mid-sized European e-commerce company. The commercial team recently introduced **free shipping on orders above €50**, starting **July 2024**. Before that, every order carried a flat shipping fee of **€5.95**.

The commercial team is enthusiastic — order volumes went up. But the finance department is skeptical. Waiving €5.95 per order is real lost revenue. They want someone to look at the data and answer one question:

> **Did free shipping actually improve profitability, or did we just give away delivery income?**

Your manager has pulled a data extract covering **January through September 2024** (three quarters) and dropped three CSV files on a shared drive for you to work with. The first six months are the "before" period; July through September are the "after" period.

She needs you to come back with a clear, data-backed recommendation in two weeks.
